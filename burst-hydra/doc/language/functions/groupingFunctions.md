___Functions___ are the way that Hydra exposes various and sundry semantics outside of 
standard expressions.

###### Grammar

    functionExpression: functionType LP  (expression (SEP expression)* )? RP;
    
    functionType:
        calendarGrainType
        | DatetimeOrdinalType
        | datetimeGrainType
        | groupingFunctions
        | nowTime
        | brioFunctions
        | cubeFunctions
        | xactionFunctions
        ;

    nowTime: NOW ;
